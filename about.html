A SHORT HISTORY OF THE VILA

Her Enduring Journey

Vila Ardi features a patio and is set in Ksamil, within just 300 metres of Paradise Beach and 500 metres of Lori Beach. This beachfront property offers access to a balcony and free private parking. The property is non-smoking and is located 200 metres from Ksamil Beach 9.

This air-conditioned apartment comes with a fully equipped kitchenette, a seating area, a dining area and a flat-screen TV. Guests can also relax in the garden.
